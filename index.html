welcome to the Farm 2 Dine

Choose Organic products as the companion to supply all the nutritions that your body needs, Yet another try to live with the rhythm of nature

All it started from our grandpa who was practising Siddha. In those days it was a stage where the diabetes , skin diseases, arthritis were booming. When my grandpa shared that the synthetic fertilizers that will travel the whole food chain (soil -> plant -> human) by being a separate chemical component as a main cause for this drastic changes that happens in the body which promotes these kind of diseases .. From him the thought started that he gave the medicine for the disease and why can't we use the food as prevention for these diseases.. “Prevention is better than cure ” instead of try to reverse the disorders we tried preventing the disorder by populating the good things that travels through food chain.

What We Offer

Our nature of business is organic farming and we are doing it at Hosur , Beththakollu area.. The base for the health is to have good food and producing good food products and make it available for more number of people is our motto and its been three years since we started organic farming by producing milk, meat, chicken, vegetables
